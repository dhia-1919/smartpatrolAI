ACTION REQUIRED — ALL MARKETS

Market-specific compliance guidelines

The AI is only as good as the standards it's trained on. Each market must provide guidelines — and patrollers must be briefed — before pilot.

What each market provides
ItemInput NeededExamplesType
T-ShirtBranding standard, old vs new logoCompliant · Violation (old branding, stained, torn, faded, unbranded)Mandatory
TrousersBranding, color requirementsCompliant · Violation (unbranded, damaged, faded)Mandatory
ShoesClosed shoe requirementCompliant · Violation (sandals, slippers)Mandatory
Jacket/CapBranding if wornCompliant · Violation (unbranded, old, torn)If Worn
HelmetBranding, conditionCompliant · Violation (cracked, unbranded)AI+Manual
WindshieldCondition, brandingCompliant · Violation (cracked, missing)Mandatory
Hair/BeardGrooming, cultural notesCompliant · Violation (untidy)Hygiene
From each market POC
1. Photos of correct kit (front+back)
2. Violation examples per item (unbranded, dirty, old, damaged, faded)
3. Strictness level per item
4. Market exceptions
5. POC sign-off before fed to AI
Why patrollers must be briefed too
If AI and patrollers judge by different rules, disagreements will be constant. Both must align on the same guidelines before pilot. Critical for accuracy AND adoption.
